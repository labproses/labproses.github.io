About Us

The Chemical Process Laboratory is under the coordination of the Department of Chemical Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Diponegoro University. The Chemical Process Laboratory is located on the 2nd Floor of Integrated Laboratory, Diponegoro University. The lecturer in charge of the Diponegoro University Chemical Process Laboratory is Prof. Dr. Moh. Djaeni, S. T., M. Eng. and Abdurrahman Fawwaz as Coordinator of the Chemical Process Laboratory Assistant. The Chemical Process Laboratory has 16 laboratory assistants and 1 laborant. Diponegoro University Chemical Process Laboratory plays an important role as an implementer of chemical process practicum which is a compulsory subject of the Chemical Engineering department, Diponegoro University.
