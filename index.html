About Us

The Chemical Process Laboratory is under the coordination of the Department of Chemical Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Diponegoro University. The Chemical Process Laboratory is located on the 2nd Floor of Building A Chemical Engineering, Diponegoro University. The lecturer in charge of the Diponegoro University Chemical Process Laboratory is Dr. T. Aji Prasetyaningrum S.T., M.Si. and Rahma Sekar Alief as Coordinator of the Chemical Process Laboratory Assistant. The Chemical Process Laboratory has 14 laboratory assistants and 1 laborant. Diponegoro University Chemical Process Laboratory plays an important role as an implementer of chemical process practicum which is a compulsory subject of the Chemical Engineering department, Diponegoro University.
